WalkDenver created the &#8220;Slow the Funk Down&#8221; yard signs as part of our Vision Zero Community Art Project, aimed at raising awareness of Denver&#8217;s commitment to Vision Zero and the goal of eliminating traffic fatalities. We partnered with local artists at the So-Gnar Creative Division, The Signtologist, and community members to design these colorful signs reminding people to please drive safely.<br />
The signs have been so popular, we&#8217;ve already given away the first 500 that we printed with grant funding. We need at least $750 to place a bulk order for more signs and get the best price. Your donation will help us meet this goal and spread the Vision Zero message in support of safer streets to neighborhoods throughout Denver.<br />
In addition to donating, you can complete this form to be added to a waiting list to receive a sign once we are able to order more. Click <a href="https://walkdenver.networkforgood.com/projects/65893-slow-the-funk-down">here</a> for more info</p>
