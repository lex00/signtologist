Dazzle was the first place I was able to show my art in Denver, curator Eric Matelski took a chance &amp; booked me here in 2004. Since then I have been apart of a few group shows, been fortunate to have a few solo shows, even had an art talk about my work, lots of great memories at Dazzle.</p>
<p style="text-align: center;">Now Dazzle has moved downtown into the historic Baur&#8217;s Building located at 1512 Curtis Street, Denver, CO 80202. The owner and curator have invited The Signtologist back to display some art as they embark on this new chapter. I am excited and honored to be showing my work once again at Dazzle! The Grand Opening is June 1st 2017 and kicks off with Otis Taylor at 6PM.</p>
<p style="text-align: center;">Founded in 1998, Dazzle has become a institution not only in Denver’s jazz scene, but the country’s, too. The club regularly touts its designation as one of DownBeat Magazine’s top 100 jazz clubs in the country. It packs in the talent to back that testimonial up, routinely booking vaunted players new and old like the Grammy Award-winning Esperanza Spalding and guitar legend Jim Hall.</p>
