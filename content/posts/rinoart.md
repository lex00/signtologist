<p style="text-align: center;">I am excited and pleased to announce that The Signtologist has been offered the wall space of <a href="http://thepreservery.com">The Preservery</a> to display artwork. I will have a consistent installation of art and will be rotating in new pieces frequently. I and am thankful &amp; happy to call The Preservery the home for The Signtologist in <a href="https://rinoartdistrict.org">The Rino Arts District</a>. Thanks again to the owners and amazing staff of The Preservery for this opportunity come join us for a drink or a meal at</p>
