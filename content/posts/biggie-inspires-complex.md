<p>Twenty-five years ago, The Notorious B.I.G. released his debut album, Ready to Die. On Friday, friends and family paused to commemorate the milestone with the unveiling of two new murals dedicated to “the livest one from Bedford-Stuyvesant.” Optimo cigar company hosted the Biggie Inspires Art Exhibit and Celebration across Clinton Hill and Williamsburg, paying homage to the homegrown MC.</p>
