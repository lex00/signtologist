<p style="text-align: center;">Denver artist Dan Ericson aka The Signtologist and his 6 year old son Omen have put together an art show.</p>



<p style="text-align: center;">Street sign paintings created by Omen and Dan are currently on display at Hazel 1581 South Pearl St Denver CO 80210.</p>



<p style="text-align: center;">Omen is home schooled and during this pandemic has been spending more time with his Dad in the art studio. The duo worked on pieces side by side using the same materials recycled signs, paint pens and reflective stickers.</p>



<p style="text-align: center;">The Signtologist has shown work around the country and has art in the American Jazz Museum and Universal Hip Hop Museum. This is Omen’s first time displaying his artwork.</p>



<p style="text-align: center;">There is no opening for this show due to Covid restrictions.</p>



<p style="text-align: center;">All seating is first come, first served</p>
