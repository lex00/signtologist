<h3>July 19 &#8211; December 31, 2020</h3>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p>This exhibit, co-curated by the American Jazz Museum and Chuck Haddix of UMKC Libraries and the La Budde Special Collections, provides an in-depth look at Parker&#8217;s brilliance and charisma, impacting the course of music like no other. KC native and pioneering composer and improviser, Charlie Parker ushered in a new era of jazz and influenced subsequent generations of musicians. This centennial exhibition features groundbreaking research, album covers, sheet music, and rare audio selections.</p>
<p>Sponsored in part by the UMKC University Libraries</p>
